Welcome

We invite Neurobiology and Neuroscience researchers to Padua to celebrate the distinguished career of Prof. Daniela Pietrobon, Full Professor of Physiology at the University of Padova. With a background in Chemistry and postdoctoral training at Harvard, her pioneering work on migraine mechanisms, calcium channels, and neurophysiology has advanced our understanding of neurological disorders. This symposium, "From Channels to Circuits," honors her career, hosting the contributions from a number of collaborators and leading scientists.
